Welcome to Pitch Practice!

Our mission is to help beginning musicians improve their ear and perfect their technique. We believe our Pitch Practice can make you Pitch Perfect!

Train your ear- Match the note you hear to the corresponding button

Pro Tip: Singers- Sit with good posture in order to breath efficiently with your diaprham.

Pro Tip- Pianists: Sit at the correct distance from the piano based on your height. This provides greater mobility.

Pro Tip- violinists: A tense left hand greatly impacts the speed at which violinists can play at. Make sure to keep you hands relaxed.

Pro Tip-Flutists: Relax! If the embouchure is too tight the sound is pinched, small and flat.

Pro Tip-Bass Guitarists: TONE IS IN THE HANDS- You can go from a dull thud to a sharp, funky punch simply by choosing where along the string you pick it and how aggressively you hit it.
